Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

LightRoll Cleaner (the "App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Important Notice: All photo data processed by this App is handled exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your photo library for the following purposes:

  • Detecting and analyzing duplicate and similar photos
  • Automatically identifying blurry photos and screenshots
  • Suggesting best shots
  • Providing photo deletion and restoration features

Processing Method: All photo analysis is performed on-device only. Photo data is never transmitted externally. Only analysis results (similarity scores, group information, etc.) are stored on your device.

1.2 Device Information

We may collect the following device information to improve app performance:

  • Device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro)
  • OS version (e.g., iOS 18.0)
  • App version
  • Language settings
  • Crash reports (anonymized)

1.3 Advertising Information (Free Version Only)

If you use the free version, Google Mobile Ads may collect the following information:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Ad impressions and click information
  • Approximate location (country/region level)

Note: While this App uses advertising identifiers, it does not track users across apps. The identifier is used solely for ad delivery optimization.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Provision

  • Photo similarity analysis and grouping
  • Automatic detection of blurry photos and screenshots
  • Best shot recommendations
  • Storage usage display
  • Deletion history management

2.2 App Improvement

  • Crash analysis and fixes
  • Performance optimization
  • New feature development
  • User experience enhancement

2.3 Ad Delivery (Free Version Only)

  • Displaying appropriate advertisements
  • Measuring ad performance

3. Data Storage and Management

3.1 Local Storage Only

All data processed by this App is stored only on your device:

  • Photo analysis results
  • Group information
  • Deletion history
  • User settings

3.2 Data Retention Period

  • Deleted photos: Kept in trash for 30 days, then permanently deleted
  • Analysis results: Retained until the subject photo is deleted
  • User settings: Retained until the App is uninstalled

4. Third-Party Information Sharing

4.1 Advertising Partners

The free version uses the following advertising partner:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob collects and uses information in accordance with its own privacy policy. Please refer to the link above for details.

4.2 Other Third-Party Sharing

We do not share user information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With user consent
  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect life, body, or property

5. Premium Version

When you purchase the Premium version, the following applies:

  • Ads are no longer displayed
  • Advertising-related information collection stops
  • Data collection by Google AdMob ceases

6. User Rights

6.1 Managing Photo Access Permissions

You can change photo access permissions at any time in iOS Settings:

  1. Open the "Settings" app
  2. Select "LightRoll Cleaner"
  3. Tap "Photos"
  4. Change access permissions ("None", "Selected Photos", "All Photos")

6.2 Data Deletion

To delete all in-app data:

  • Uninstalling the App will delete all local data
  • Manually emptying the trash will clear deletion history

6.3 Managing Ad Personalization

You can limit ad personalization in iOS Settings:

  1. "Settings" → "Privacy & Security"
  2. "Apple Advertising"
  3. Turn off "Personalized Ads"

7. Security Measures

This App implements the following security measures:

  • All data processing completed on-device
  • Data storage using iOS secure storage
  • Minimized external communication (only for ad display)
  • Regular security updates

8. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian discovers that their child is using this App, please contact us at the address below. We will respond promptly.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated without notice due to legal changes or service improvements. In case of significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or on this page.

10. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Japan. Any disputes regarding this App shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the court having jurisdiction over the developer's location.
