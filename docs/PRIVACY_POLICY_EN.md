## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Photo Data

The App accesses your photo library to detect duplicate and blurry photos.

- **Method of Access**: We access your photos only when you explicitly grant permission.
- **Purpose**: Photo data is used solely for similarity analysis, blur detection, and duplicate identification.
- **No External Transmission**: **Your photo data is never sent to any external server. All processing is performed entirely on your device.**
- **Storage**: Only analysis results (such as similarity scores and blur detection results) are stored locally on your device. Photo data itself is never copied or stored separately.

### 2.2 Advertising Information

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect the following information:

- **Advertising Identifier (IDFA)**: Used for ad delivery purposes.
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, screen size, etc.
- **Usage Data**: Number of ad impressions, clicks, etc.
- **IP Address**: Used to determine approximate location (country/region level).

**Details**: Please review Google AdMob's Privacy Policy at:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 2.3 App Usage Statistics (Optional)

The App may collect the following information to improve quality:

- **Crash Reports**: Error information when the app crashes.
- **Usage Data**: Feature usage frequency, screen transitions, etc. (anonymized statistical data only).

This information is **anonymized** and cannot be used to identify individuals.

### 2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does **not** collect:

- Your name, email address, or phone number
- Location data (GPS)
- Contacts, calendar, messages, or other personal data
- Any other personally identifiable information

## 5. Data Storage and Deletion

### 5.1 Storage Location

- **Photo Data**: Stored only on your device (iOS Photo Library). Not stored on our servers.
- **Analysis Results**: Stored locally on your device (app's local storage).
- **Trash**: Deleted photos are moved to iOS's "Recently Deleted" folder (automatically deleted after 30 days).

### 5.2 Retention Period

- **Analysis Results**: Retained until you manually delete them or uninstall the App.
- **Advertising Information**: Subject to Google AdMob's retention policies (typically several months to 2 years).

### 5.3 Data Deletion

You can delete your data using the following methods:

1. **Uninstall the App**: All local data will be deleted.
2. **Reset from Settings**: Reset analysis results from the App's settings screen.
3. **Reset Advertising ID**: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Reset Advertising Identifier.
