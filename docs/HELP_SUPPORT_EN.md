## 1. App Overview

LightRoll Cleaner is an app that automatically detects duplicate and blurry photos from your iPhone's photo library and helps you organize and delete them.

### System Requirements

- **OS**: iOS 18.0 or later
- **Devices**: iPhone, iPad
- **Languages**: Japanese, English (more languages planned)

### Key Highlights

- **Fully Local Processing**: Your photo data is never sent to external servers
- **High-Accuracy Detection**: Proprietary algorithms for precise duplicate and blur detection
- **Safe Deletion**: Trash feature prevents accidental deletion (30-day recovery available)
- **Auto Scan**: Automatically scans on a regular schedule
- **Premium Features**: Ad-free and unlimited scans for a comfortable experience

## 2. Key Features

### 2.1 Duplicate Photo Detection

Automatically detects exact and near-identical photos.

- **Detection Targets**: Multiple copies of the same photo, before/after editing versions, etc.
- **Detection Accuracy**: Similarity threshold adjustable in Settings (default: 85%)

### 2.2 Blurry Photo Detection

Automatically detects out-of-focus and camera-shake photos.

- **Detection Targets**: Out-of-focus, motion blur, camera shake, etc.
- **Detection Accuracy**: Blur threshold adjustable in Settings (default: 0.3)

### 2.3 Similar Photo Grouping

Groups similar photos together for easy review and batch deletion.

- **Grouping Criteria**: Minimum group size configurable (default: 2 photos)
- **Display Format**: Organized by date and similarity

### 2.4 Trash Feature

Deleted photos are moved to iOS's "Recently Deleted" folder.

- **Recovery Period**: Photos can be restored at any time within 30 days
- **Permanent Deletion**: Automatically permanently deleted after 30 days
- **Caution**: Recovery is not possible after 30 days

### 2.5 Auto Scan

Automatically scans your photos on a regular schedule to detect duplicates and blur.

- **Scan Frequency**: Choose from daily, weekly, or monthly
- **Notifications**: Receive notifications when scans complete (can be disabled in Settings)

### 2.6 Notification Feature

Receive notifications for the following events:

- Scan completion
- Low storage capacity warnings
- Reminders (periodic cleanup prompts)

**Quiet Hours**: Set times when you do not want to receive notifications (default: 10 PM - 7 AM)

## 3. User Guide

### 3.1 Initial Setup

1. **Launch the App**: Open LightRoll Cleaner
2. **Grant Permissions**: Allow access to your photo library
   - You can choose "Selected Photos" or "All Photos"
   - Granting access to more photos improves detection accuracy
3. **Enable Notifications (Optional)**: Allow if you want to be notified when scans complete

### 3.2 Scanning Photos

#### Manual Scan

1. Tap the **"Start Scan"** button on the home screen
2. Scanning begins (takes seconds to minutes depending on the number of photos)
3. Results are displayed after scanning completes

#### Auto Scan

1. Go to **Settings** > **Notification Settings** > Enable **Auto Scan**
2. Select scan frequency (daily, weekly, or monthly)
3. Scans will run automatically going forward

### 3.3 Reviewing and Deleting Photos

1. Review groups of duplicate/blurry photos on the **Scan Results** screen
2. Tap each group to view details
3. Select photos you want to delete
4. Tap the **"Delete"** button
5. Confirm by selecting **"Delete"** in the confirmation dialog

**Important**: Deleted photos are moved to "Recently Deleted." You can restore them from the trash within 30 days.

### 3.4 Restoring from Trash

1. Open **Settings** > **Other** > **Trash**
2. Select photos you want to restore
3. Tap the **"Restore"** button
4. Photos will be returned to their original location

### 3.5 Customizing Settings

The following options can be adjusted from the **Settings** screen:

- **Analysis Settings**: Similarity threshold, blur threshold, minimum group size
- **Display Settings**: Theme (Light/Dark), app icon
- **Notification Settings**: Enable/disable notifications, quiet hours, auto scan frequency
- **Permission Management**: Re-configure photo library and notification permissions

## 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1. Are my photos sent to external servers?

**A1**: No, they are never sent externally. All processing is performed entirely on your iPhone. Your privacy is fully protected.

---

### Q2. Can I restore accidentally deleted photos?

**A2**: Yes, you can restore photos from the trash within 30 days.
- Open **Settings** > **Other** > **Trash**
- Select the photos you want to restore and tap **"Restore"**

**Caution**: After 30 days, photos are permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. We recommend backing up important photos.

---

### Q3. What is the difference between Free and Pro?

| Feature | Free | Pro |
|---------|------|-----|
| Duplicate Photo Detection | Limited | Unlimited |
| Blur Detection | Limited | Unlimited |
| Advertisements | Displayed | None |
| Scan Count | Limited | Unlimited |
| Deletion Count | Limited | Unlimited |
| All Premium Features | No | Yes |

See [Pricing Plans](#6-pricing-plans) for details.

### Q4. How long does scanning take?

**A4**: It depends on the number of photos:
- **1,000 photos**: Approximately 10-30 seconds
- **5,000 photos**: Approximately 1-3 minutes
- **10,000+ photos**: Approximately 3-10 minutes

Scanning runs in the background, so you can continue using other features.

---

### Q5. What types of photos are detected as "blurry"?

**A5**: The following types of photos are detected:
- Out-of-focus photos
- Camera-shake photos
- Motion blur (blurred moving subjects)

**Note**: Intentionally blurred photos (such as panning shots) may also be detected. Please review before deleting.

---

### Q6. Can I adjust detection accuracy?

**A6**: Yes, go to **Settings** > **Analysis Settings** to adjust:
- **Similarity Threshold**: Higher values detect duplicates more strictly (default: 85%)
- **Blur Threshold**: Higher values detect blur more strictly (default: 0.3)
- **Minimum Group Size**: Minimum number of photos for grouping (default: 2)

---

### Q7. Can I cancel my subscription?

**A7**: Yes, you can cancel at any time:
1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone/iPad
2. Tap **[Your Name]** > **Subscriptions**
3. Select **LightRoll Cleaner**
4. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

After cancellation, you will continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period.

---

### Q8. How do I change photo library permissions?

**A8**: You can change permissions using the following methods:

#### From Within the App
1. Open **Settings** > **Other** > **Permission Management**
2. Change **Photo Library** permissions

#### From iPhone Settings
1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone/iPad
2. Select **LightRoll Cleaner**
3. Tap **Photos**
4. Change permissions (None, Selected Photos, All Photos)

---

### Q9. Can I disable notifications?

**A9**: Yes, you can disable them using the following methods:

#### From Within the App
1. Open **Settings** > **Notification Settings**
2. Turn off **Enable Notifications**
3. Or disable individual notifications (scan completion, storage warnings, etc.)

#### From iPhone Settings
1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone/iPad
2. Tap **Notifications**
3. Select **LightRoll Cleaner**
4. Turn off **Allow Notifications**

---

### Q10. What should I do if the app is not working?

**A10**: Try the following steps:
1. Force quit the app and relaunch it
2. Restart your iPhone
3. Update the app to the latest version
4. If the issue persists, please [Contact Us](#7-contact-us)

## 5. Troubleshooting

### 5.1 Scan Does Not Start

**Causes and Solutions:**

1. **Photo library permission not granted**
   - Go to **Settings** > **Other** > **Permission Management** and allow photo library access

2. **No photos in library**
   - Confirm that photos are saved in your photo library

3. **App is not up to date**
   - Update to the latest version from the App Store

### 5.2 Scan Stops Midway

**Causes and Solutions:**

1. **Insufficient memory**
   - Close other apps to free up memory
   - Restart your iPhone

2. **Background processing stopped**
   - Keep the app in the foreground while scanning

3. **Very large photo library**
   - Scanning may take longer for libraries with 10,000+ photos. Please be patient

### 5.3 Cannot Restore Deleted Photos

**Causes and Solutions:**

1. **More than 30 days have passed**
   - Per iOS specifications, photos are permanently deleted after 30 days
   - We recommend backing up important photos in advance

2. **Permanently deleted from trash**
   - Photos permanently deleted from trash cannot be recovered

### 5.4 Ads Are Not Displaying

**Causes and Solutions:**

1. **No internet connection**
   - Enable Wi-Fi or cellular data

2. **Using an ad blocker**
   - Disable your ad blocker (ad revenue helps sustain app development)

3. **Pro plan is active**
   - Ads are not displayed for Pro users (this is normal behavior)

### 5.5 App Crashes

**Causes and Solutions:**

1. **Update to latest version**
   - Check for updates in the App Store

2. **Restart your iPhone**
   - This may resolve temporary issues

3. **Reinstall the app**
   - Your data is stored in the photo library, so reinstalling will not affect your photos

4. **If the issue persists**
   - Please [Contact Us](#7-contact-us)
